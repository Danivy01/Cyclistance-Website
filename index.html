About us

Cyclistance Inc. founded in 2022, Cyclistance is a specialized collective focused on the emergent needs of the expanding cycling populace. Our inception coincides with the significant proliferation of cycling activities, particularly accelerated by the COVID-19 pandemic. Recognizing an unmet need in the market, we are committed to developing comprehensive mobile solutions aimed at enhancing the safety and well-being of cyclists.
